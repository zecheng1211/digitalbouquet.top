FAQ

Digital bouquet frequently asked questions.

Answers about free digital bouquets, virtual flowers, flower meanings, and bouquet making.

Is digitalbouquet.top free?

Yes. You can create a free digital bouquet with at least three flowers and a custom greeting.

What is a digital bouquet?

A digital bouquet is an online flower gift that combines virtual flowers, a message, and a beautiful animated result.

Can I choose flower meanings?

Yes. Read the flower meaning guide before choosing roses, orchids, tulips, lilies, sunflowers, and more.

Does it work for birthdays?

Yes. A happy birthday digital bouquet is one of the most popular uses for the site.
