Create guide

How to create a free digital bouquet online.

Follow four simple steps: choose an occasion, select at least three flowers, write a greeting, and complete an animated virtual flower bouquet.

Start making a bouquet

1. Choose the occasion

Select birthday, anniversary, sympathy, thank you, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, graduation, or another scene. The occasion shapes the tone of your digital bouquet.

2. Choose flowers

Pick at least three flowers from roses, orchids, tulips, peonies, lilies, sunflowers, daisies, carnations, hydrangeas, lavender, irises, daffodils, and more.

3. Write the greeting

Add a happy birthday digital flower message, romantic wish, thank-you note, sympathy line, or custom greeting template.

4. Complete the bouquet

Preview an animated digital flower bouquet that feels polished and shareable, then refine the words or flowers if needed.
