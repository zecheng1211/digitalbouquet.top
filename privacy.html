Privacy

Privacy policy for digital bouquet users.

We aim to make online flower gifting simple while respecting user privacy.

Information and bouquet content

digitalbouquet.top lets users create a digital bouquet with flower selections and greeting text. We design the experience to be simple and privacy-conscious. If you contact us by email, we use your email address to respond to your question about digital bouquets, flower meanings, virtual flower bouquet features, or support.

How information is used

Information is used to improve the free bouquet maker, answer support requests, and understand which flower meanings, occasions, and greeting templates are useful. We do not ask for delivery addresses because a digital bouquet is shared online.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@digitalbouquet.top.
